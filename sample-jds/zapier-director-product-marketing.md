We believe all Zapiens should be rewarded competitively and equitably, using practices that are simple and transparent. This philosophy ensures we’re able to find, grow, and retain exceptional people from a broad range of backgrounds. Here’s how we define our compensation principles:

Competitive: Zapier pays well among the technology sector.

Equitable: Consistent pay practices; Pay for impact

Simple: Pay is well understood, and pay practices are built for scale.

Transparent: Zapiens know how pay works, including how their pay is determined.

A Candidate's compensation package is finalized once the interview process is concluded and accounts for demonstrated experience, job knowledge, skills,  abilities, and internal equity. We use a business impact approach to base pay, which means we set pay for all Zapiens based on their demonstrated impact on Zapier’s success. In alignment with that philosophy, the upper half of a pay range is typically reserved for individuals who have consistently demonstrated a high impact in their current role and level while at Zapier.

Zapier is hiring a Director of Product Marketing to lead our next phase of growth. This is a strategic and hands-on leadership role. You’ll own how we position Zapier to buyers, evolve our GTM motion to match rapid product innovation, and manage a team at the edge of AI-driven marketing.

You’ll partner closely with Product, Engineering, Sales, and Marketing leadership to define where we play, how we win, and what our true moat is. You’ll move the team from traditional, campaign-based launches to a more continuous, insight-driven GTM engine, one that keeps pace with how fast we’re shipping.

If you’re someone who can zoom out to define strategy and zoom in to rewrite a deck, challenge a product roadmap, or jump into a sales deal, you’ll thrive here.

About You

8+ years of experience in product marketing or related roles, with meaningful time spent in B2B SaaS and enterprise GTM

Led and developed high-performing teams, ideally managing managers or senior ICs, and know how to raise the bar while moving fast

Both strategic and hands-on. You can define positioning at the company level and also get into the details of messaging, decks, and campaigns

Strong product intuition. You’re comfortable debating with Product and Engineering on roadmap priorities, differentiation, and long-term moat

Understand enterprise sales deeply. You’ve partnered closely with Sales, influenced deal strategy, and built GTM motions that support complex, multi-stakeholder buying processes

Experience evolving GTM models before, moving from big launches to more iterative, continuous release cycles

Excited about AI, not just as a feature, but as a fundamental shift in how products are built, marketed, and sold

High bar for clarity and quality, and you push teams to communicate simply, even when the product is complex

What You’ll Do

Define and drive positioning and narrative: Establish clear, differentiated positioning for Zapier’s platform and products, grounded in customer needs

Shape product strategy and moat: Partner with Product and Engineering to influence roadmap decisions, ensuring we’re building toward a durable competitive advantage

Transform our GTM motion: Evolve the team from traditional launch cycles to a continuous, release-driven model aligned with product velocity and sales needs

Align with enterprise sales: Build tight partnerships with Sales leadership to ensure our messaging, packaging, and enablement directly support pipeline and revenue

Lead and develop the PMM team: Manage a team of ~7 PMMs, setting a high bar for performance, clarity, and impact while creating space for speed and experimentation

Drive AI-forward marketing: Push the team to adopt AI in how we create, test, and scale messaging, while also shaping how we position Zapier in an AI-first world

Ensure consistency across the funnel: Partner with Brand, Content, and Demand Gen to deliver a cohesive, compelling story from first touch through close
